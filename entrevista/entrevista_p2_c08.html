03 💬 Vampiro
¿Te lo puedes imaginar, esta espléndida situación doméstica, el padre vampiro que vela con cariño al hijo vampiro mientras los dos atesoran esa pequeña dosis de sueño del diablo? —El vampiro rió de repente; era una risa cálida y suave—. ¿Y Lestat sentado en el otro extremo de la habitación, mirándola también él?
"Can you imagine it—this splendid domestic scene, the vampire father fondly watching over the vampire child while both treasure that small measure of the devil's sleep?" The vampire laughed suddenly; it was a warm, soft laugh. "And Lestat sitting at the other end of the room, watching her too?"

"But we must leave here!" said the Claudia of years later, suddenly, as if the old memories were nothing more than a cobweb she brushed away with a wave of her hand. "We must go to Paris. Louis—the city of light, the city of mortals who live and love beautiful things."

06 💬 Louis / Claudia
—Perdóname —dije con la mayor amabilidad posible, retirándome lentamente de aquella visión hasta que la habitación que me rodeaba recuperó toda su concreción de madera, alfombras, papel y velas—. Así que hay vampiros en París —le dije yo. —Vampiros que son como nosotros —dijo ella—, vampiros con mentes y corazones. No criaturas de la tierra.
"Forgive me," I said as gently as possible, withdrawing slowly from that vision until the room around me recovered all its concreteness of wood, carpets, paper, and candles. "So there are vampires in Paris," I said to her. "Vampires who are like us," she said, "vampires with minds and hearts. Not creatures of the earth."

08 💬 Louis / Claudia
—Sólo porque estamos cansados y las cosas son difíciles —le dije a ella y a mí mismo—. El teatro, la ópera, los museos. Todo lo que nuestra especie ha creado y conservado por siglos para nuestra alegría y enriquecimiento. —Y los vampiros —repitió ella—. ¡Los vampiros de París!
"Only because we are tired and things are difficult," I said to her and to myself. "The theatre, the opera, the museums. Everything that our species has created and preserved for centuries for our joy and enrichment." "And the vampires," she repeated. "The vampires of Paris!"
