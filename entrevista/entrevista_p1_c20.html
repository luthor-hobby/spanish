Primera parte — Pasaje 20

El día de Todos los Santos. Claudia lleva crisantemos al cementerio. Vuelve con una pregunta que paraliza a Louis: '¿Cuál de vosotros me hizo lo que soy?' Claudia confronta a Lestat: '¿Cómo lo hiciste?' Lestat la amenaza. La paz queda destruida. Lestat sospecha. Claudia se acerca a Louis. '¿Me lo dirás? ¿Cómo fui hecha?' Louis confiesa que no lo sabe.

§ La pregunta de Claudia

—¡Para las dos que dejaste en la cocina! —dijo Lestat con furia. Ella lo miró por primera vez, pero no dijo nada. Se quedó allí mirándolo como si nunca le hubiera visto. Y entonces dio varios pasos hacia él y lo miró, todavía como si lo examinara positivamente. Me moví hacia adelante. Podía sentir su ira. Su frialdad. Y ahora ella se volvió hacia mí.
"For the two you left in the kitchen!" said Lestat with fury. She looked at him for the first time, but said nothing. She stood there looking at him as if she had never seen him before. And then she took several steps toward him and looked at him, still as if examining him positively. I moved forward. I could feel her anger. Her coldness. And now she turned toward me.

"I saw him looking at her! He was as mortal as she was and as I was, equal. Why, if not, this size, this form? Why am I a girl? I spoke her name. I think I wanted to distract her. It was impossible. The tide had come loose. Lestat's eyes lit up with acute fascination, malign pleasure. "You made us like this, didn't you?" she accused him. "What you are?" he asked.
