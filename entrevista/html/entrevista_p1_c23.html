"She didn't succeed, did she?" the boy asked softly. "What do you honestly think?" asked the vampire. He had settled back in his chair. He looked at the boy intently. "That she was . . . as you said, destroyed," said the boy; and he seemed to feel the words, so that he swallowed after he'd said the word destroyed. "Was she?" he asked.
