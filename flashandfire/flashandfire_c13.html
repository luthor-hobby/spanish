PRESENTING THESSALAR the FANTASTIC: DARE to be DAZZLED!! The marquee gleams brightly against the night sky of a trendy night club, offering its patrons an exhilarating and singular experience. Thessalar the Fantastic is a magic act, and he is in rare form tonight. Before the astonished gaze of the crowd, he pulls a dove out of his hat, vanishes it again, and does tricks with interconnected hoops, playing cards, and flash paper. He makes a pair of salt shakers dance across a table, and cuts a lime into wedges without touching it. He seems to pass into and out of a mirror at one point, and the whole time keeps up a lighthearted dialogue as if the whole audience is a gathering of his closest intimates. By the end of the night he takes his bow to thunderous applause, and eventually makes his way backstage. He stops by the bar to order a glass of rum and coke. He cuts a striking figure in a three piece suit with tails and a top hat. Dark-blond hair peeks from beneath the hat, and his teal eyes sweep over the crowd. He's done well enough that several people come over to talk to him, and Thessalar welcomes the attention. He has always thrived on the kindness of strangers. Outside, his car is parked on the edge of the lot. It is an unremarkable yellow Kia Soul. Through the windows, the backseat looks to have a few discarded fast food wrappers and a duffel bag.
