01 💬 Lex
Mientras Thess duerme, algo se mueve entre sus sueños. O quizás así lo percibe, mientras Bodhi observa su rostro apacible y deshace el trabajo de Mona, el olvido forzado. Lo desteje, como quien tira de un hilo para deshacer un jersey. Un momento para sopesarlo y... casi listo. Una cosa más. Se adentra en el cerebro de Thess para buscar el código de desbloqueo de su teléfono. Cuando Thessalar despierta, Bodhi se ha ido. 9:47 PM ---------------- 9:48 PM Akaji recibe un mensaje de un número desconocido. Necesitamos hablar antes de que vayas al almacén.
While Thess is asleep, something moves through his dreams. Or he experiences it that way, perhaps, as Bodhi watches his peaceful face--and undoes Mona's work, the enforced forgetfulness. Unweaving it, like pulling on yarn to unravel a sweater. A moment to consider and--almost done. One more thing. He reaches into Thess's brain, for the unlock code for his phone. And when Thessalar wakes up, Bodhi's gone. 9:47 PM ---------------- 9:48 PM Akaji gets a text, from an unknown number. We need to talk before you go to the warehouse
vocabulario
destejeunweaves — from destejer, to unweave/unpick; cf. tejer, to knit/weave
se adentra enreaches into, goes deep into — adentrarse en = to go deep into, enter the interior of
olvido forzadoenforced forgetfulness — olvido = forgetting/oblivion; forzado = forced
gramática
  • O quizás así lo percibe — Epistemic hedge: 'or perhaps he experiences it that way'. Quizás + indicative (rather than subjunctive) signals the narrator leans toward this being true.
  • como quien tira de un hilo — Como quien + infinitive: 'like someone who pulls on a thread'. Impersonal comparison using quien with indefinite reference.

03 💬 Lex
No lo recordarás si te lo digo. [pin de ubicación: hora y lugar. Un parque público, poco concurrido, de día]
You won't remember if I tell you [pin for a time, place. Public park, sparsely populated, daytime]
vocabulario
poco concurridosparsely populated, not very crowded — concurrido = busy/frequented; poco concurrido = the opposite
pin de ubicaciónlocation pin — a location share via messaging; ubicación = location
gramática
  • No lo recordarás si te lo digo — Futuro simple en la prótasis negativa: unusual — more standard Spanish would use present indicative (si te lo digo) in the conditional clause. The future in the si-clause here mirrors a casual, slightly colloquial register.
04 💬 Sheikh
Sofisticado, pero bueno. Si eres quien creo que eres, no debiste escribirme.
rich but ok. If this is who I think, you shouldn't have texted me.
vocabulario
sofisticadohere used ironically as 'fancy/slick' — lit. sophisticated; in colloquial register can imply pretentiousness
no debisteyou shouldn't have — deber in preterite = should have (unfulfilled obligation in the past); cf. no deberías haber for a fuller past contrary-to-fact
gramática
  • Si eres quien creo que eres — Cláusula de identidad con relativo: 'if you are who I think you are'. The embedded quien creo que eres is a relative clause; note indicative in both clauses since this is a factual (open) conditional.
